Dissertation / MSc Finance, Lancaster

Which valuation model actually predicts price

Built dividend discount, discounted cash flow, residual income and price to earnings models in Stata, with CAPM cost of equity by industry and a two year forecast horizon. Tested all four against true outcomes across more than 13,000 firm years. The earnings multiple model explained 56% of price movement against 11% for discounted cash flow.
